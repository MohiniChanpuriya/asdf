Master of Business Administration

__________________________________________

MBA is one of the most popular post-graduate courses in India and abroad.
The full form of MBA is Master of Business Administration.
For most of the managerial level jobs across domains and sectors an MBA degree
. MBA admission is based on MBA entrance exams followed by
a personality assessment test round
(Group Discussion or GD, Written Ability Test or WAT and Personal Interview or PI)
. undergraduate degree from any stream in order to pursue an MBA course.
The most popular MBA entrance exams are CAT, GMAT, XAT among others.
IIM Ahmedabad, IIM Bangalore, IIM Calcutta, IIM Lucknow, FMS Delhi,
XLRI and ISB are some of the top MBA colleges in India which take
admission via these exams followed by individual GD-PI rounds.
The MBA fee generally ranges between INR 10-25 lakh depending on college to college.









Pharmacy


About Pharmacy

__________________________________________

Diploma in Pharmacy (D. Pharm): Candidates should have completed higher secondary
education with science subjects i.e. Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Mathematics.

Bachelor in Pharmacy (B. Pharm): Candidates must have completed their higher
education from the national- or state-level education board. They must have
studied Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics or Biology and English subjects.
They must complete 17 years of age on December 31 of the year of admission.

Master of Pharmacy (M. Pharm): Candidates who have completed B. Pharm degree
with minimum qualifying marks as required from a pharmacy college as
approved by the Pharmacy Council of India (PCI).

For Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm. D): For a six-year course, candidates must
have completed higher education from any state/national education board.
They must have completed 17 years of age in the year of admission.









About Us

Babulal Tarabai (BT) group of Educational Institutions, under registered society LSES (Little Star Education Society, founded in 1993). This group consists of “BTIRT (Babulal Tarabai Institute of Research and Technology 2008), BTIE (Babulal Tarabai Institute of Excellence2006), BTIPS (Babulal Tarabai Institute of Pharmaceutical Science 2019), BTM (Babulal Tarabai Mahavidyalaya 2019) and LSSM (Little Star Shailesh Memorial Senior Secondary School Sagar 1992)”, progressing under the chairmanship of visionary Mr. Santosh Jain ‘Ghari’. Little Star Education Society was founded with a pious objective of providing quality education for the benefit of society and the nation as a whole. In the year of formation of education society, it established “Little Star Shailesh Memorial Senior Secondary School providing education to children from Nursery to 12th standard.
